## What is TellIT?

TellIT is a powerful yet simple Kanban board designed to streamline your project management process. Whether you're working solo or collaborating with a team, TellIT helps you visualize tasks, track progress, and stay organized.

## Getting Started

1. Create a new board for your project.
2. Add columns to represent your workflow stages (e.g., To Do, In Progress, Done).
3. Start adding tasks to your board and move them across stages as they progress.
4. Collaborate with your team, assign tasks, and track everyone's progress in real-time!
